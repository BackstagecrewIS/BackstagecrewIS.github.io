Harvest Pale

Castle Rock Brewery

abv 3.8%

Harvest Pale is brewed with a gently-kilned malt, and an aromatic blend of American hops added during the brewing process gives this pale beer exceptional poise. Its distinct hop flavour leads to a crisp finish.

Elsie Mo

Castle Rock Brewery

abv 4.7%

A single malt ale with a lovely golden hue. A blend of First Gold, Challenger, Aurora and Bobek hops give Elsie Mo its delicate citrus aroma and floral taste

Preservaion

Castle Rock Brewery

abv 4.4%

Brewed to the traditional East Midlands style, Preservation is an easy-drinking full flavoured best bitter. Well-rounded with some residual sweetness and balanced with resinous hop character.

US IPA

North Riding Brewery

abv 3.8%

A US IPA dry hopped with Centennial and Calypso. Flavours and aromas are floral, lemon and peardrops.

Captain Hopbeard

Totally Brewed

abv 5.5%

This pacific pale ale is saturated with Australian and New Zealand hops to deliver tropical flavours of pineapple and passion fruit with a background of spice and molasses

Bridge Bitter

Burton Bridge

abv 4.2%

Amber coloured bitter beer with a lingering aftertaste. Challenger, Target and Northdown hops with Styrion added at the end of the boil give a dry hoppy aroma with a fruity background.
